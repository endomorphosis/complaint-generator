Before You Begin

What a client should have ready

  • A working email address for account recovery and case continuity.
  • A short description of what happened, who was involved, and when the key events occurred.
  • Any documents, screenshots, notes, or messages that may support the complaint later.
  • An idea of the harm suffered and what relief may be requested.
